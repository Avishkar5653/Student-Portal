About Student Portal

The Student Portal is designed to provide students with a centralized platform for accessing academic information, announcements, course materials, and institutional services.

Our goal is to improve communication between students and educational institutions while providing easy access to learning resources and important updates.

This portal demonstrates modern web development practices and collaborative development using Git and GitHub workflows.
